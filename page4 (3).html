Internet and technology (1980 - present )

From bulky, localized mainframes in the 1980s, technology and the internet evolved into a ubiquitous global infrastructure. This digital revolution transformed personal computers and dial-up connectivity into an always-on mobile era driven by cloud computing, high-speed wireless networks, and artificial intelligence.

The Present: AI and UbiquityToday, artificial intelligence, augmented reality, and seamless Internet of Things (IoT) integrations are deeply woven into daily life. With more than half of the global population connected, high-speed satellite internet and integrated AI assistants dictate the next frontier of modern information technology.
